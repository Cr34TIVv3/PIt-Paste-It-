How many pastes can I create?

The short answer is: as many as you like! The are however some limitations if you don't have an account: your paste will be only public, you can't create 'private' pastes and will not be avaible for more than 30 days.

Duration of a paste?

Your pastes can not be unlimited. However, you can select from the "Paste expiration" the longest period of time.

Can I change my username?

Yes, you can from the account panel. You might want to change some other details, like password or email address.

In how many programming languages can i type?

This topic is work in progress. We intend to add as many languages as we can. Currently, we have 3 languages that support syntax highlight (C++, Haskell, Java).

How can I contact Pastebin?

Just check out our 'Contact page' and send us your message.

How we can support you?

That's a nice question:) We wont ask you to donate money for us, but you can simply help us by contactic us and sending frequently feedback

How can I get content removed?

You are only able to remove items that you created while you were logged in. If you paste something as a guest, there is no option for delete. The pastes will expire in 30 days.
